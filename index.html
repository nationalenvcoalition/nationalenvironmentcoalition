Help the Amazon right now

Every action we take to protect the world’s forest makes a difference.

The fires have created a layer of smoke estimated to be 1.2 million square miles wide, and the loss of forest is devastating for mitigating global warming. The rainforest is meant to absorb carbon, not produce it.

And while it might seem devastating, and not enough to be on your mind, here is why it should be. Here are some facts and ways you can make an impact and help.

3 easy ways to help protect the Amazon

Donate to NEC’s Fund for Amazon Fires


Every dollar you donate will go directly to restoring and recovering the worlds largest rain forest. Forest are essential to our survival. They filter the air we breathe and water we drink. Today trees are being lost at a rate of 26 football fields per minute.

Educate and voice the importance of the Amazon

The Amazon produces more than 20% of the worlds oxygen, why is referred to as the “lungs of the planet!” There are approximately 10 million species of animals, plants and insects known to man and more than half of them call the rainforest their home.

Be a safe environmental consumer

Some purchases can hurt the Amazon. Learn that there are solutions to climate change and forest fires just in what you choose to buy. Products made from fossil fuels can I have a huge detrimental impact on our environment. Buy forest made products that have FSC on the label. This ensures that The products were made with safe Amazon Forest standards called The Forest Stewardship Council
